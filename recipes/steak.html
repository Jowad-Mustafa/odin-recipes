Description

A deliciously prepared grass fed ribeye steak, seared in avocado oil, and basted in garlic rosemary butter

Ingredients

  • Salt and black pepper
  • Garlic powder
  • Grass fed ribeye steak
  • Grass fed butter
  • Organic rosemary
  • Avocado oil
  • Crushed garlic cloves

Instructions

  1. Cover steak in avocado oil and cover in salt, pepper, and garlic powder
  2. Sear in avocado oil and bring to and internal temp of 115 ºF
  3. Add crushed garlic, butter, and rosemary and baste until an internal temp of 130 ºF
  4. Rest for 8min and slice
