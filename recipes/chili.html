Description

Ingredients

  • Ground beef
  • Tomato sauce, paste, and crushed tomatoes
  • Chopped onions
  • Pinto beans, pink beans, and dark kidney beans
  • Packs of chili seasoning

Instructions

  1. Saute onion and brown beef together in a big pot
  2. Add all other ingredients and 1 can of water
  3. Bring to a boil and simmer for 15min
